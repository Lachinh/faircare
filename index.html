Questions

Common questions

What is Faircare?

Faircare shows what the same medical procedure costs at different facility types across South Carolina, so you can find the lower price before you book.

Where does the price data come from?

Prices are drawn from hospital and clinic price transparency files that facilities are required to post publicly.

Do I need an account?

No. Searching is free and does not require a login or insurance connection.

Why is the same procedure priced so differently?

Hospital outpatient departments are generally allowed to charge facility fees that independent outpatient clinics cannot, even for the identical procedure code.

Which parts of South Carolina do you cover?

The Midlands, Upstate, Lowcountry, and Pee Dee regions today, with more being added.

Is this medical advice?

No. Faircare shows price information only. Always follow your doctor's guidance on which care you need.
